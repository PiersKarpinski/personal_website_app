Entrepreneur

I'm an entrepreneur

I see things and think of ways they could be done differently, more efficiently, better.

Areobooks was founded in the spring of 2014. Our vision is simple: put authors and publishers in control of how their e books are published and distributed; remunerate them according to the actual cost of publishing and distributing e books. Afterall, the cost of selling 1 copy of an e book is roughly the same as the cost of selling 1 million copies of that e book.



The name

Areobooks shares the first four letters of its name with Areopagitica, a tract written by John Milton (you know, the guy who wrote Paradise Lost) opposing licensing and censorship of the press. Areobooks shares Milton's belief that the greatest benefits come from people having access to the greatest number of ideas.

Our mission

Authors and publishers should be at the centre of e books and that means handing them the reigns. Authors and publishers who choose to sell e books through Areobooks have complete control over how their e books are priced and marketed, and how they choose to connect with their audience.

Why now?

E books represent 15% of the UK publishing market. As the technology behind e books improves that number will rise. However, the e book marketplace is dominated by print book thinking. Areobooks is needed to shake-up that marketplace. Doing so will create a fairer and more efficient environment for all participants.
