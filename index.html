Hi my name is Piers

A Little About Me

I am a qualified lawyer, junior web developer and budding entrpreneur. I'm based in London and currently enjoying the Indian summer of my mid-20s.

I have always been a fan of words. I enjoy reading them for pleasure and analysing them for meaning. Two of my favourite books are Alice's Adventures in Wonderland and Through the Looking-Glass by Lewis Carroll. My fascination with words is one of the reasons I became a lawyer and probably one of the reasons I find back-end web development difficult!



More About Me?

I like trying things. I believe that you only get one life so you should do what you love and that you shouldn't settle for anything less. I think people worry too much; one of my favourite quotes is from Seven Years in Tibet, when the Dalai Lama tells Brad Pitt that "If a problem can be solved there is no use worrying about it. If it can't be solved, worrying will do no good".

What I've Done

I was lucky enough to go to Oxford University where I studied History. I've tried my hand as an English language teacher, first in Kenya and then in Venezuela. I dabble in web development and I have spent a number of years training to be and being a lawyer (if law's your thing, see the Lawyer section for more details).

Where I'm going

This is the part I'm excited about.

You can get hold of me via email (piers.karpinski@gmail.com), LinkedIn, Facebook or Twitter. You're also quite likely to find me wandering around the parks in London (though not lonely as a cloud). See the Contact Me section for more details.
