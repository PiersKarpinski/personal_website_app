Entrepreneur

I'm an entrepreneur

I often see things and can't help but think of ways they could be done differently, more efficiently, better. I like to be part of that process of improvement.

Areobooks was founded in the spring of 2014 for these reasons. Our vision is to replace the outdated models on which the creators of books are rewarded, creating a more efficient model that appropriately reflects the realities of e book publishing and distribution, and which represents a clean break from the old world of print books.



What's in a name?

Areobooks shares the first four letters of its name with Areopagitica, a tract written by John Milton (you know, the guy who wrote Paradise Lost) opposing licensing and censorship of the press. Milton's vision is no different to that of Areobooks: that the greatest benefits come from people having access to the greatest number of ideas.

What is Areobooks?

Areobooks is an e book publishing and distribution website that puts authors and publishers in control of the content they have created. Control in terms of price, marketing and how they connect with and add to their audience. Areobooks does all this without taking any cut of the sale price of any e book sold.

Why now?

E books currently represent 15% of the UK publishing market. E book market share continues to grow, though some are suggesting that it is beginning to plateau. Areobooks will help invigorate the pace of change and allow authors and publishers to take advantage of the benefits that technology is bestowing upon them.
